Dallas Cowboys

The Dallas Cowboys are one of the most iconic brands in all sports. They’re known not only nationwide, but around the world. Experiencing a game at AT&T stadium should be on every sports fan bucket list. If you’re interested in learning more about them, check out the following fun facts about the Dallas Cowboys.

Fun Facts about the Dallas Cowboys!

#1 – The Dallas Cowboys franchise was granted on January 28, 1960! Just last year they celebrated their 60th year as a team.

#2 Since their founding, they’ve won FIVE Superbowl’s, which ties them for 3rd place with the San Francisco 49ers. And they follow The Pittsburg Steelers and New England Patriots who are both tied with six wins.

#3 They’ve won 8 NFC championships. Unfortunately, the bulk of those was in the ’70s to ’90s. They have not won an NFC championship since 1995.
